# App

Purrsistence is a Time Management / Motivation / Habit Tracker App to achieve your goals.

---

## 🚀 Track Your Progress
Start getting things done by tracking your time progress on the goals you want to achieve and earn more rewards the longer you stay focused.

---

## 🐱 Collect Rewards
Collect different cats and items and see your hard work paying off by having an even greater collection of cats.

---

## 👥 Socialize
Socialize with your friends and see how they're doing with achieving their goals.

---

## 📊 Weekly Statistics
See statistics on how well you did on a weekly basis.
